My Dream for Dystopia

I long that one day, we will be free from the confines of a rigged Republic and a suffocating Capitalist regime. No, but like, seriously. I really want all out anarchy. Not Mad Max folley, but like…. Waterworld…. or Willow. Fuck Star Wars though. I would rather suffer in the Sarlack pit than have to deal with that confusing mess. You’re either living in a Dictatorship, or in a society of people with stick-up-your-butt-itis. Like really, people that wanna be a Jedi are either asexual or incels, and anyone that thinks they can be a Sith clearly forgot about the rule of two, and also, who wants to live with the anxiety of constantly looking behind your back? BUT FUCK THAT THOUGHT, because literally no matter what or who you think you can be in the Star Wars universe, you’re gonna get murdered, and it’s gonna be painful and not fun, possibly fucking terrifying.

Thousand year digestion.

Suffocation and freezing in space.

Being eaten by anything or anyone.

Acid.

Having your limbs ripped from your body by shear force.

Breaking the law, or just pissing someone off.

WHAT THE FUCK ISN’T ILLEGAL IN THIS GODDAMN UNIVERSE ANYWAY?!?!
