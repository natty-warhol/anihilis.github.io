Getting Away

Sitting here on day 1 Million or so of our COVID trials, I watch Calvary, a film from 2014 with an all star cast of european actors (I don’t dare assume where they’re all from, I’m American and stupid). The film is about a Priest who is forced into a situation where a man angry at the Catholic church decides to kill a good priest for attention because of all the evils the bad priests have done. None of this really matters, beyond the fact that there’s a portion in the movie where the Priest decides he wants to go to Dublin for a visit.

GAHHHHHHHHHH.

Being locked away is no good, but what other option do we have? All I can think about is how all my life I’ve wanted to find a home away from civilization so I can live in privacy and peace alone.

Is that cliche to say?

On the bright side, my chances of getting out there do seem higher these days. With the dire circumstances we are in, I see property prices in other places going down, and a more likely chance of us (my partner and I) moving out there…. Wherever there may be.

I dunno, I just really wanna find peace and solitude, yo. I’m too cynical and too emotional to live in this world I feel like. Solitude is the only way I feel peace anymore.
