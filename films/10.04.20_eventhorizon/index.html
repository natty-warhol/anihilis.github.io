10.04 - Event Horizon

Event Horizon (1997)

Damn does this movie get a lot of slack for it’s kind of shitty ending and all, also it kinda goes into the substanceless a bit; but I love it! It’s a campy mess! Also the effects are pretty alright considering when it came out.
“A rescue crew investigates a spaceship that disappeared into a black hole and has now returned…with someone or something new on-board.”, starring Laurence Fishburne and Sam Neill, this movie, I feel, takes advantage of these two actors beautifully.
It does most things wrong, from telling instead of showing most things, even explaining the plot at the very beginning of the movie because it has to do with worm-holes and shizz; to terrible character creation by, you know, telling each other who and what each other is known for so we’re all on the same page.
Anyways, I can’t go on any further talking about this movie cause it’s kind of a confused mess with no real cohesive story. However, if you like a good sci-fi that kinda leans into hell-raiser territory, I highly recommend this one.
