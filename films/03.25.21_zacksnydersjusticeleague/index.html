03.25 - Zack Snyder’s Justice League

Well, everyone’s talking about it..

Holy crap, I get the hype though. This remux is way more than just an alternate version like you’ve seen in the past. This is a whole new retelling of the story. If you’ve seen the horrendous version that DC thought would work, then you’d probably still have a bad taste in your mouth like I did. Once you get over how upsettingly boring and embarrassing that version was, I emplore you to try this version out.

It’s a four hour movie. It doesn’t even feel like a four hour movie, however. It’s segmented into multiple parts, like chapters. These chapters are perfect capsules that really format this movie to make you feel like you’re simply binge-watching a favourite television show.

The changes made to this movie are so very uncanny. There are still clips from the original movie, but they are put in places in the movie that are far removed from where they were in the former. Maing one wonder, which scenes were actually intended to be the way they were seen in the original cut, and how close to the original imagining this new cut is.

It’s too big to really go into detail without writing a whol novel about it. I can say though, that people you didn’t think had a single place in this movie (Cyborg, Flash, Gordon, literally anyone, really), actually have motivations, and a backstory in the version.

If you don’t care about DC, then don’t watch this, but if you like Batman, or hero movies, the definintely watch this movie, even if you saw the original cut, this will fix your feelings.
