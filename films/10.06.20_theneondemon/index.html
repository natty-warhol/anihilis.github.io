10.06 - The Neon Demon

The Neon Demon (2016)

Another wonderful film by Nicolas Winding Refn. This guy knows what’s up.
Starring the highly esteemed Keanu Reeves, and the young Elle Fanning (almost uncomfortably so), this one is about a lot of things, and I feel it’s up to your interpretation where you take it. Elle Fanning plays Jesse, an underage (fittingly so, because she was underage by a year as well while acting this movie.. they even had to wait to film some scenes –oof–) girl run away from home, hoping to make it in modeling, because in her eyes, shes doesn’t have any skills, but she can get what she wants from being pretty. The entire movie generally kinda just follows her journey through interviewing with an agency, to making it big; while doing so, breaking up with her partner, risking getting murder-ish raped by Keanu Reeves, and a couple other notable events.
I would definitely recommend watching this movie, I don’t really want to give too much of it away because I really think it’s worth seeing and coming to a conclusion of your own what you see. Though I can’t promise you won’t see nothing, because there are a lot of reviews out there saying the movie was just visual porn.
Anyways, I love this one. Go see it. Farewell.
