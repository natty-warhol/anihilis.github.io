Mac ‘n Cheese

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Pasta
  • 3 (preferred, but less is okay) cheeses of choice
  • Milk (thicker the option, the better)
  • Lime juice
  • Spices

    • Pepper
    • Garlic
    • Salt
  • Beef
  • Panko or other bread options

Instructions

Bring well salted water to a rolling boil, placing pasta once ready; make sure to stir to avoid sticking to the sides or bottom.

Preheat the oven to 450Â°.

Heat the Skillet for the beef (med-high), place butter to lubricate the pan (Â½ tbsp), once heated (we want it pretty hot) place in beef, breaking down first, then let sit, only stirring occasionally (to ensure browning, and a little crunch).

Grate the cheeses, starting with 1 Â½ cups, and grating more to necessity. Once complete, set some aside for later.
